2024 First/Access Platform Update




Welcome to the 'First/Access' page! First/Access is a platform launched in 2023 that allows early access to all sorts of tools and applications released by BlockchainUniversity, geared towards helping all generations learn about blockchain technology.


The BlockchainUniversity team is currently in the process of building the first, one of a kind decentralized operating system for compatible smartphones that can be jailbroken (Android 9 or under). As this is still the beginning of our journey, we are aiming to release a early, baseline version of the smartphone OS by Q2 in 2025.
